Clear aside the paywall with Readr

Readr handles the paywall on news articles so that you can focus on reading what you want! It is your pass to the amazing journalism on the web.

How it works

Dowload the Readr Chrome extension and subscribe

You get 20 article passes with a $10 monthly subscription

Read any article on a supported site

Readr will use 1 article pass to auto-log you in through any paywalls you hit.

Get more credits at any time

Whenever you run out of credits, click the button in the extension to easily get more!

Support journalism

Every time you use a Readr article pass, 75% of the revenue from the pass goes straight to the publisher!
